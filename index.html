VSP Source Locator

This is a utility to calculate the location of a single or selection of VSP sources. It calculates source X and Y coordinates from the offset and azimuth from the wellhead and vice versa. The calculations are not unique to a VSP survey and it could be used to calculate the distance between any two fixed points.
